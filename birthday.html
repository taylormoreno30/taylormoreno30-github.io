Birthday Cake Protein Bars for breakfast & dessert

What do you NEED to make your loved ones the best bday gift?
  • 1 1/4 cup oats (blend to oat floar)
  • 1/2 cup ashew butter
  • 1/3 cup honey (or blend dates with a little almond milk until thick liquid sweetner form)
  • 1/4 cup vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • rainbow sprinkles
  • melted coconut oil (optionl)
How?

Blend oat flour, combine ingredients, knead until flour is worked through (use your hands). Add melted coconut oil if the texture is off and flour needs extra moisture. Press “dough” evenly into a pan. Refrigerate. Cut into bars
