About Me

Get to Know Me!

I am Maddie Hoffer from Long Island, New York. Growing up I was a competitive gymnast and both my parents worked. So, my sisters and I took care of ourselves a lot after school. Most nights I had to cook for myself and eat without my family since my gymnastics practices started so early and ended so late. That may sound sad, but I loved the independence and freedom the kitchen gave me. I have always been conscientious of what I put in my body. And I never say no to dessert and sweets. I strongly believe in moderation and balance in all aspects of life. Today as a DI women's lacrosse player, I can't stress how important it is to find time to give my body the nutrients it deserves. Plus baking and cooking is fun! Creating new dishes encourages me to connect and share with the people I love.
